Bevel

Bevel is the first end to end shaving system designed to reduce and prevent razor bumps and irritation, a common problem for people of color. The Bevel razor is a single blade safety razor that promises to reduce your razor bumps and prevent irritation for those with coarse, curly hair.

Milk and Brookies

We first heard about this young company on an episode of the Shark Tank. Milk and Brookies is the brain child of Jovon English, along with her partners, Naimah Harris and Tisa Smart-Washington. Milk and Brookies is a mash-up of a brownie and cookie, hence the name Brookie. They offer specialty flavors such as red velvet, blondie, peanut butter, snickerdoodle, and much more. Brookies can be ordered online and are the perfect dessert for a company christmas party or gift
