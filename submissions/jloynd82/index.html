Some of my hobbies...


By Jake Loynd

Snowboarding

In the winter I enjoy going out to Hidden Valley and snowboarding with my sibblings. I have been snowboarding since I was in forth grade and over the years I have become quite good at it. My favorite part is racing down the hills and going off jumps.

Golf

During the summer I play golf with my friends and family. Although I am not the greatest at the game, it is always fun to play and spend time with friends.

Hiking

During the spring, summer, and fall, I go hiking at local parks with friends and family.
