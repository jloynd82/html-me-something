Kud

Two years ago, Kud followed my boyfriend into his apartment. We thought he was a girl because he didn't have balls. It turns out he was part of a catch and release program, and since he wasn't chipped, we kept him. He proceeded to infest the apartment with fleas and knock over anything around the house with liquid in it. Nowadays, he stomps on my head and meows while I'm sleeping because he wants to go outside where he chirps at birds he don't know how to hunt. And I love him. <3 <3 <3

Kitties Gone Bye Bye

Samosa (and Spaulding)

Samosa was my cross-eyed cat. My ex and I got him and his sister, Spaulding, from Tenth Life to scare away the big rat and little mice who lived in our first St, Louis residence several years ago. Upon adoption, the ex and I agreed that if we ever broke up, I would get Samosa, and he would get Spaulding. One day Samosa never came home. My ex and I broke up shortly thereafter. Maybe I'm superstitious, but beautiful baby must've sacrificed himself so I could break the curse of that shitty relationship. And I miss them.<3 <3 <3
