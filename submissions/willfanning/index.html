Education and Career

Education

I attended college at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and graduated with a BS in Chemistry in Dec. 2011. The concentration of my degree was in Environmental Chemistry. Life science and the study of the natural world remains an academic interest of mine.

Career

After graduating I began working as an Analytical Chemist at a pharmaceutical testing laboratory. My job duties included classical wet chemistry and instrumental analysis of pharmaceutical compounds. Lab work required lots of documentation, attention to detail, and precision in your actions (all good traits to carry on towards programming).

I signed up for the Summer of Code program with the intent of breaking in to the local technology sector. I hope to find interesting and challenging work in areas where science and computer software interact.

A Cool Trip

Stonewall, CO

In southern Colorado, west of Trinidad and east of the Sangre De Christo Mountain Range is a town called Stonewall, appropriately named for the large stone wall formation that slices through town and the surrounding area. I am lucky enough to visit a ranch here every two years. Most recently, I went back in June 2016. It's one of the most beautiful places I've seen and I look forward to returning.
