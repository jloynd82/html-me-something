I grew up playing drums, and not in the most typical way a drummer is thought of these days.

Jazz was the music of choice for me, and to this day, it is my favorite genre and style of playing.

Here is a list of my top five jazz songs I beleive everybody should listen to at some point in life:

  1. "All Blues" - Miles Davis
  2. "Take Five" - Dave Bruebeck
  3. "Autumn Leaves" - Cannonball Adderley
  4. "Moanin'" - Art Blakey
  5. "Blue Train" - John Coltrane

It just so happens that these top five songs are also performed by my top five jazz musicians.

This list is just the beginning, there is so much more to discover...
