3 CATS AND A DOG

Margaret

Margaret, the Fantastic Dealer of Wounds, is 9 years old. She's a tough broad from the streets of Philadelphia,PA. I got her as a scroungy kitten from a scary shelter for $1.00. Now she's gorgeous, with her long hair and dilute calico markings. She rules our house.

Hank

Hank Mardukis is 4 years old. He's a soft suburban cat from St.Peters, MO. My husband picked him out at a shelter because he was the calmest kitten and he was orange and white. We got Hank when we moved in together so Margaret would have a playmate/someone to beat up.

(Liz) Lemon

(Liz)Lemon is most likely about a year old. She's a bad a$$ kitten from the streets of Overland, MO, literally. She's the newest addition to our little family. She ran in front of my car on Woodson Road, so we brought her home. She was full of fleas and purrs. She has a beautiful tabby swirl pattern.

Chewbacca

Chewbacca is 2 years old. He's a bundle of love from rural Hawk Point, MO. He is our only dog, but Hank is his best friend, tied eith everyone he's ever met(except Margaret and Lemon). Chewie is a pug/border terrier mix who looks like Chewbacca, except when he gets a haircut- then he looks like Wicket. He is a very good dancer.
